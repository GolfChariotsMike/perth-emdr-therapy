About Nicole Hann

Endorsed Counselling Psychologist & Founder of Perth EMDR Therapy

🌸

Nicole Hann

Nicole is an endorsed Counselling Psychologist who has been in private practice for 10 years working with a range of individuals, couples and families. Nicole has a special interest utilising EMDR and other trauma informed interventions to address trauma, childhood trauma and relational difficulties.

Nicole also has extensive experience working with parents and women in the perinatal period, as well as chronic pain and grief and loss.

Nicole has been building Perth EMDR Therapy with the hope of continuing EMDR long into the future. This became her focus after experiencing ongoing positive results from using EMDR with clients.

Nicole's vision for EMDR Therapy Perth is to create a space that is warm, welcoming and professional. She is currently in the process of recruiting like-minded Psychologists and EMDR therapists who share the same therapeutic values, and who believe that the therapeutic alliance is the foundation for effective treatment and meaningful change.
